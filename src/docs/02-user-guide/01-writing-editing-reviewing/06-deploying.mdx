# Step 6: Deploying the final content

![Editorial workflow Step 6](./assets/Editorial_workflow_6.png)

The merged content will first be deployed to a staging environment (UAT) for final testing and review, before being pushed to the production site.
This step ensures that the end-users always see the most polished and accurate version of your documentation.
